About me

I have Bachelor's degree on Social Sciences and master's on Sociology, at the Federal University of Goias. I'm PhD student at Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, on the department of History of Science and Technics and Epistemology. My research permeates the studies of the sciences, especially of the computer sciences. My goal is to map the groups that promote education and greater inclusion of women in this area, with those that characterizes themselves as feminists, in Brazil, and to record their methods of sharing knowledge, in counterpoint to the way we promote it within universities.

On the other hand, I work with digital security training for activists. I'm also sysadmin in some activists collectives.

CV

Cl4ndestina

Cl4ndestina is a project of running servers to host and provide infrastructure to social movements and feminists collectives. Steffania and I started this project on 2016.

Segue Passeio

Segue Passeio is a blog where I post my notes, translations and my research on the doctorate. Follow my profile at Academia.edu

Digital Security Trainer

I've being working in the design and facilitation of information security processes for activists and feminist groups. These processes seek to nurture the power of ways to feel secure, rather than basing and nurturing the culture of fear.

Other interests

I am producing materials, articles and guides to support and help people in the tech field. Some of these materials can be found at my medium page.

I'm interested in increase the connection and dialogues with activists and feminists groups to think the way of production and share of knowledge. Besides, I want to create new methodologies of security, and produce new cosmologies as well.

Previous Projects

I have been part of many projects that promotes technology and free software in a more acessible way in Brasil.

Mulheres na Tecnologia (cofounder) is group of producing and promoting space for women in tech.

Xanta (cofounder) is a collective that offers space to learn more about linux's servers and free software. It uses to be a collective of hosting to social and activist movements.
